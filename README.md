# Customer-churn

Tools: Excel | SQL | Python | Power BI | Tableau

Domain: Subscription / SaaS Analytics

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Customer churn is a critical business challenge impacting revenue and growth. This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior, engagement, payments, and support interactions to identify churn drivers and high-risk customers. An end-to-end analytics solution was built, transforming raw customer data into actionable insights and executive-ready dashboards.

BUSINESS OBJECTIVES

Measure overall customer retention health
Identify churn rate and revenue impact
Detect high-risk customers early
Understand behavioral drivers of churn
Support data-driven retention strategies

KEY INSIGHTS

Customers with low tenure and low engagement have the highest churn
Payment failures and frequent support tickets strongly correlate with churn
Month-to-month contracts churn more than long-term contracts
High-risk customers contribute significantly to potential revenue loss

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS

Improve onboarding for early-tenure customers
Proactively target high-risk customers with retention offers
Reduce payment failures through alerts and flexible payment options
Enhance support response times to improve customer satisfaction
